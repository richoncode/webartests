Static Background Prior (EMA)

Background Modeling

This POC verifies the Selective Processing architecture by maintaining a persistent background model.

EMA Math:
Bₜ = (1-α)Bₜ₋₁ + αCₜ
Where α is the learning rate. Low alpha creates a stable background; high alpha adapts quickly to changes.

Foreground Mask: Computed as the difference between the current frame and the background model, thresholded to identify moving players.
