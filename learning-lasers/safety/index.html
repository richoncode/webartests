⚠️ THE PVC DEADLINE

Never laser PVC (Vinyl) or Leather tanned with Chrome. Heating these materials releases Chlorine Gas, which combines with moisture in your lungs to form Hydrochloric Acid. It also corrodes your laser's optics and mirrors within hours.

The "Specular" Reflection: A laser beam hitting a shiny surface like polished brass doesn't just "stop." It bounces with 90% of its power intact. Always ensure the IR shield is down on the F2 or you are wearing the correct goggles for the material.
