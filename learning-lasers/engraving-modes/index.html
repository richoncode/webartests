Advanced Pathing Concepts

Crosshatch (90°)
Performs two passes: one horizontal and one vertical. This "traps" heat and ensures deep, dark engravings on materials that might have directional grain.
Dot Duration
In "Dot" or "Bitmap" modes, this controls how many microseconds the beam dwells on a single coordinate. Higher duration = more "thump."
Outline vs. Pulse
The F2 preview can show just the bounding box or the actual path. Use "Pulse" preview to see exactly where the galvanometers will be steering.
LPC Overlap
At 1000 LPC, lines are 0.01mm apart. Since the spot is 0.03mm, you are hitting the same spot 3 times per pass. This is high thermal stress.
