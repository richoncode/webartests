Components

Callouts

Used for explanatory notes, tips, and warnings inside content pages. Always use <strong> for the lead phrase.

Blue — informational. Explains a concept, links to context, or adds background understanding.
Green — success / usage tip. How to use the output, positive outcome, or next steps.
Orange — caution. A trade-off, limitation, or something to watch out for.
Red — warning. Data loss, destructive action, or hard constraint.
